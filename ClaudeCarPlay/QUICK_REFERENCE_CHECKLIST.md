### Week 6: Outreach (Feb 10-16) ⬜

- [ ] Engage actively on social media

### Week 7: Final Prep (Feb 17-23) ⬜
- [ ] Final Kickstarter page review (5 people)
- [ ] Submit page for Kickstarter staff review
- [ ] Set up email automation (5 emails)
- [ ] Schedule launch day social posts
- [ ] Run through launch day plan (rehearsal)
- [ ] REST on weekend!

**Done when:** Everything ready, page approved, you're well-rested

### Week 8: LAUNCH! (Feb 24 - Mar 2) ⬜
- [ ] GO LIVE Monday 9 AM EST
- [ ] Execute launch day plan (hour-by-hour)
- [ ] Post first backer update within 24 hours
- [ ] Respond to ALL comments within 30 min
- [ ] Goal: 100+ backers, funding goal hit in 72 hours

## LAUNCH DAY ESSENTIALS

### Pre-Launch (8:00 AM) ⬜
- [ ] All devices charged
- [ ] Coffee/tea made
- [ ] Open all necessary tabs
- [ ] Set up notifications
- [ ] Take deep breath

### Go Live (9:00 AM) ⬜
- [ ] Click "Launch Project"
- [ ] Tweet announcement
- [ ] Post to Instagram + LinkedIn + Facebook
- [ ] Email subscriber list
- [ ] Post to Reddit (r/SideProject, r/CarPlay)
- [ ] Post to Hacker News "Show HN"

### First Hour (9:00-10:00 AM) ⬜
- [ ] Send emails to Tier 1 journalists (10)
- [ ] Reply to EVERY comment
- [ ] Thank first backers
- [ ] Monitor all social channels

### Throughout Day ⬜
- [ ] Respond to comments every 30 min
- [ ] Post updates every 2-3 hours
- [ ] Email outreach in waves
- [ ] Engage on social media
- [ ] Celebrate milestones (10, 25, 50 backers)

### End of Day (9:00 PM) ⬜
- [ ] Post "Day 1 recap" update
- [ ] Log metrics in spreadsheet
- [ ] Thank backers publicly
- [ ] Plan tomorrow's strategy
- [ ] GET SLEEP
